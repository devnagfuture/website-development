Companion to the Menstrual Revolution

We educate women and girls on maintaining proper hygiene during their menstrual cycle, guiding them on the best and most sustainable products to use. Our focus is on promoting the menstrual cup as an eco-friendly alternative to disposable sanitary pads, which contribute significantly to plastic waste. Menstrual cups are not only environmentally friendly, reducing plastic pollution and water consumption, but also highly economical, as they can be used for up to 10 years.

To support underprivileged communities, we donate menstrual cups to girls' hostels and women in need. Our initiative is implemented through educational seminars in schools, colleges, corporate sectors, and residential societies. We also raise awareness by organizing bike rallies, bicycle marathons, and debates to encourage open discussions and drive positive change.

Health Checkup Camp and Awareness

We organize breast cancer and cervical cancer checkup camps, along with awareness programs
for couples and adults to educate them about sexually transmitted diseases (STDs).
Our goal is to prevent individuals from becoming carriers of such diseases and to promote
early detection and prevention.

We also provide education on HPV (Human Papillomavirus) infection, raising awareness about how
to protect against this virus, which can lead to various cancers, including:

  • Cervical cancer
  • Penile cancer
  • Anal cancer
  • Vulvar cancer
  • Vaginal cancer

In addition to awareness initiatives, we organize HPV vaccination
camps to help protect individuals from these life-threatening diseases and promote a healthier future.

Devmai – Promoting Our Culture and Values

In today’s fast-paced world, finding time to dedicate years to learning traditional art forms can
be challenging, and the high costs of Arangetram often make it unaffordable for many.
Understanding these challenges, Kalasurya Foundation is offering a comprehensive crash course
in the rich heritage dance style of Bharatanatyam, making it accessible to all.

Our program is designed to take students from basic to advanced levels in just
one year, ensuring they gain a deep understanding of the art form without the long commitment.

To make this cultural treasure more accessible,
we offer the entire Arangetram program for just ₹21,000, allowing more aspiring dancers to embrace
our heritage art form without
financial burden.

Through this initiative, Kalasurya Foundation is dedicated to promoting
Dharma, Shastra, and our cultural heritage, inspiring the next generation to connect with and carry forward
the timeless beauty of Bharatanatyam.

project 08

Sayukt Parivar Sanman (Joint Family Awards)

The Kalasurya Foundation firmly believes in the philosophy of "No More Old Age Homes." In line with this vision, we honor joint families by presenting them with awards to inspire others to embrace the joint family system instead of the nuclear family setup.

Learn More

Sayukt Parivar Sanman (Joint Family Awards)

Living in a joint family can be challenging, but it offers numerous benefits.
In contrast, nuclear families often struggle to provide adequate care for
elderly family members, leading to an increase in admissions to old age homes.

Through our initiative, we aim to promote the joint family culture, thereby
reducing the number of elderly individuals seeking shelter in old age homes.
This effort will also help address societal issues such as depression, stress, and loneliness among senior citizens.

By encouraging families to stay together and support one another,
we strive to build a more connected and compassionate society for future generations.
